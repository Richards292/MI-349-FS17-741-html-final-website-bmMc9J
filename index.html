Star Treaders

The sky is NOT the limit

Eyes on the stars?

Look no further! You've found Star Treaders, a local company dedicated to the development of affordable space travel. We're bringing people the wonders of space to those who never expeted it.

What can Star Treaders do for you?

Whether or not you're a student, researcher, or simply a space enthusiast, Star Treaders has something for you. We're constantly researching ways to make research and tourism in space more affordable. That isn't an easy feat, and we're still working at it! In the mean time, we're offering grants to the average citizen. We're making it our mission, to get you to the stars.

We have grants available to everyone willing to apply, regardless of age or location. In the application process, we favor students, researchers, and company partnerships when considering who goes. Follow our links at here or at the top of the page for more information about each group.
