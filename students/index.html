Meet the Students!

Description

These are students in their undergraduate or graduate career looking to broaden their horizons, literally. Often strapped for cash, students would likely be very eager to experience the solar system from a new perspective.

Quote from a student

"I've always liked the idea of going to space! However, I can barely afford to keep myself in school, so that isn't really an option at the moment. I'd apply to any grants or scholarships for the oppurtunity though!"

Who is it?

These deals would be offered to undergraduate seniors and graduate students. The expected age would be between 20 and 26. Scholarships would not be restricted based on location.

Goals and attitudes

Star Treaders will provide you, the student, with a unique environment with which to hone your skills. We strongly believe allowing students to step out of their comfort zone and into space will give them a chance to grow as individuals and academics.

You can expect us to handle all of the planning and simply invite you into a prepared environment, then let you do as you wish. You can expect some degree of direction, but will mostly be self-guilded. we will be pairing you with willing researchers, which will be beneficial to both parties.

Ammenities

You will be provided with a unique environment to hone your skills as a young academic. The facility you will be working in is equipped with state of the art laboratories, entertainment facilities, excersize rooms, and more! You will not find yourself searching for something to do when you're with Star Treaders
